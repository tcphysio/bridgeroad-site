When you arrive

Bridge Road Physiotherapy is located inside Uplift Gym at 507 Bridge Road, Richmond. Enter through the main Uplift Gym entrance.

If you arrive early, wait near reception and I'll come out to meet you. If the door is locked, or you are unsure where to go, call or text 0458 007 583.

Please wear comfortable clothing if possible, and bring any relevant scans, reports, referrals or care plans.
