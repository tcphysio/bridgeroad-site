The way professional athletes are looked after, for people who just want to keep moving.

You do not need to be elite to train like it matters. Most people who get hurt need an accurate diagnosis, a plan that builds real capacity, and someone who explains it without jargon. That is the whole job, and it is what you get here.

Sports and cricket injuries

  • Clear diagnosis first. We work out what is driving the problem before treating it.

Running injuries

  • Load and capacity assessed against your training history.
  • A return-to-running plan with weekly targets you can follow.
  • Tissue tolerance built for the demands running actually places on you.
  • For runners from a first 5k through to a marathon.

Gym-based rehab

  • Rehab with load, using the gym next door rather than guesswork.
  • Keep training in some form where possible, not complete rest.
  • Measured and re-tested, so progress is real and visible.
  • For lifters and gym-goers who do not want to simply stop.
