Services

What we treat

Six common reasons people come in. The thinking is the same across all of them: find the real problem, build capacity, get you back to the activity, and reduce the odds of it returning. Each section covers the common problems, how treatment works, what to expect, and who it suits.

Sports injuries

Common problems. Acute and overuse injuries from team and individual sport: hamstring strains, ankle sprains, shoulder and knee problems, tendon issues.

How treatment works. We diagnose the injury, settle the irritable phase if there is one, then rebuild strength and capacity specific to your sport before we clear you to return.

What to expect

A clear diagnosis, a staged plan, and return-to-sport criteria you can measure against, not a guess.

Who it suits

Club and recreational athletes who want to return to performance, not just to pain-free walking.

Gym & lifting injuries

Common problems. Shoulders, lower backs and knees that flare up under load, often from technique, programming or a sudden jump in volume.

How treatment works. We look at the movement, not just the sore spot. We modify load so you keep training, fix the driver, and progress you back to full lifts.

What to expect

Most people keep training in some form throughout. The goal is to load again with confidence.

Who it suits

Gym-goers and lifters who don't want to be told to simply stop and rest.

Running injuries

Common problems. Shin, knee, calf, Achilles and hip pain, usually linked to training load, capacity or mechanics.

How treatment works. We assess your running capacity and history, manage load sensibly, and build the tissue tolerance the sport demands. Running retraining where it helps.

What to expect

A return-to-running plan with weekly targets, and a clear sense of why it happened.

Who it suits

Runners from first 5k to marathon who want to keep running while they fix the problem.

Strength-based rehab

Common problems. Persistent or recurring problems that haven't responded to passive treatment or rest alone.

How treatment works. Structured, progressive loading using the gym next door. We measure, progress and re-test rather than guess.

What to expect

Measurable gains in strength and capacity, and a body that tolerates what you ask of it.

Who it suits

Anyone whose problem keeps coming back, and who is ready to do the work.

Return to sport

Common problems. The gap between feeling better and being ready to compete, where most re-injuries happen.

How treatment works. We use objective criteria, not just time, to decide when you're ready. Strength, capacity and sport-specific testing.

What to expect

A confident, criteria-based return, with the risk of re-injury managed rather than ignored.

Who it suits

Athletes coming back from a significant injury or surgery who want to return once, properly.

Physio for active adults

Common problems. The aches, niggles and setbacks that come with staying active through a busy life and the years.

How treatment works. Practical assessment, a plan that fits your week, and education so you can manage minor flare-ups yourself.

What to expect

Less time sidelined, and the knowledge to handle small problems before they grow.

Who it suits

Busy adults aged 25 to 55 who want to keep moving without a setback every season.
