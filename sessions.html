Sessions

What happens when you come in

What happens, what it costs, and what you leave with.

Your first session

Initial consultations are 45 minutes. Most of it is understanding your problem before jumping to treatment.

  • We talk. What happened, what aggravates it, and what you want to get back to.
  • I assess. Movement, strength and specific tests that point to a diagnosis.
  • I explain. What I think is going on, how confident I am, and what it means for your timeline.
  • We plan. Treatment on the day where useful, plus a short plan to start with.

Follow-up sessions

Follow-ups are 30 minutes. This is where we re-test, progress the plan, troubleshoot, and shift toward return to training or sport.
